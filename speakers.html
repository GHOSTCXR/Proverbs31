Meet Our Speakers

Dr. Olufunmilola Mojoyinola

She is a Nigerian consultant and a gynaecologist. She has made significant contributions not just in medicine, but in cervical, publicity awareness of breast cancer and screening. Due to her diligence and passion in healthcare, she was the emerged leader of the prime honour roll award. her crave for excellence made her share her experience in educating healthcare professionals and also to the public about the early detection and preventive strategies. She is currently the Director of Clinical services at Ajeromi General Hospital, Lagos. Her dedication to work remains a testimonial to healthcare and also her advocacy for women's health and wellbeing.

DR LOVE OGAH

Dr Love Jesutofunmi Ogah is a prominent Nigerian religious leader and an advocate for purposeful living. She is the founder of the Bloom Woman Initiative designed to equip women to thrive spiritually. She hosts the Love Ogah Podcast, covering Christian living, marriage, identity, and destiny with several impactful episodes since late 2024. Her teachings often revolve around faith, spiritual resilience, marriage preparation, and navigating courtship under spiritual guidance.

Evangelist Titoluwa Sam-Oladapo (Tito The Writer)

She is a Nigerian author, speaker and founder of TSO [ TITOLUWA SAM OLADAPO Ministries]. She founded TSO Ministries in 2008, with the aim to transform and also, following mandate of spreading the Gospel. Her Ministry combines prophetic teachings, inspired spoken words and writings, and the organisation of global outreach campaigns; all with the vision that entails " Till every eye sees Jesus ". She has written and published books like “Focus” and “Cascading Waters”.

Mrs Funto Ibuoye

She is a Nigerian entrepreneur, author, speaker and interior designer known for her impactful work in empowering and transforming women. She is the founder and CEO of five28, an interior design company and property development firm. She bagged a second-class upper degree in Accounting from Covenant University in 2010; she also became a chartered accountant in 2011. In her early career, she worked as an accountant in the tech industry before becoming a brand manager at L’Oréal, a multinational beauty company. She created the beautified network, an initiative aimed at allowing women to discover in the fullness of God's given purpose and live it. The beautified network stresses on empowering women to pay attention to their potential and contribute meaningfully and positively to the society.
